Conditions and Warranties

Converting images to mosaics is a very imprecise science. Therefore this application is to be treated as a 'toy' and must not be relied upon for it's accuracy. Payments are made for the necessary processing or manufacture, not for the instructions which may contain errors (especially at right and bottom edges).
